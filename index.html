About SatohiJr

SatohiJr (SATJR) is a community-first meme token on the BNB Chain (BEP-20). It was created for fun and culture-building, prioritizing transparency and simplicity over complex token mechanics.

There are no guarantees or expectations of profit. The goal is to foster a positive meme culture, make everything clear and public (distribution, treasury, liquidity), and let utilities and initiatives evolve with community input.

SatohiJr is an independent project not affiliated with any other cryptocurrencies, brands, or trademarks.
